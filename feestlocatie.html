How to get to Weilandshof

Public transport

Weilandshof is easily reachable by bus 410 that drives from "Rooseveltplaats" to "Wijnegem Molenheide". From here it's only a 2 min walk to the wedding venue "Weilandshof". The last bus back to Antwerp leaves around 00h59. After this time it's best to take a taxi. You can ask the waiters at the wedding venue to call a taxi if necessary. We recommend to share a taxi with others to split the cost as taxis can be expensive.



Taxi

You can also come to the weddding venue by taxi. Here are the contact details of some taxi companies:

  • Antwerp-Tax: +32 3 238 38 38
  • Antwerpen Taxi Centrale: +32 3 369 58 58

Prices start at €2.95 (+ €2.5 for night trips) + €2.10 per kilometer.

Note that Uber and Lyft are not available in Belgium.
